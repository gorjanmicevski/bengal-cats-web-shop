About Us

Origins
Welcome to PetCare, a family-owned Bengal cattery located in Skopje. Our love for Bengals began when we adopted our first kitten, Gucci, and fell in love with her unique personality, striking appearance, and playful nature. Growing up with a veterinarian as a father and being surrounded by animals, we have always had a deep appreciation and love for animals.

Becoming Breeders
As our passion for Bengals grew, we decided to become breeders and provide others with the joy of owning a Bengal cat. At PetCare, we are dedicated to breeding healthy and well-socialized Bengal kittens that will make excellent pets. Our cats are kept in a home environment and are given plenty of love and attention to ensure they are well socialized.

Medical Care
We take great pride in our breeding program and ensure that all of our cats meet our strict standards for health, temperament, and appearance. We work closely with our veterinarian, and our cats receive regular check-ups and vaccinations.

Why choose PetCare?
When you choose PetCare as your Bengal cattery, you can rest assured that you are getting a beautiful and healthy kitten that has been bred with love and care. Our cats are raised in a home environment and given plenty of love, attention and medical care to ensure that they are well-socialized, healthy and ready to become part of your family. We take great pride in our breeding program and are committed to providing our customers with the best possible experience.
